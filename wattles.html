Australian Native Plants - Wattles

# Name Common Description
1 Acacia acanthoclada Harrow Wattle The Harrow Wattle is a dense shrub with prickly foliage and greyish bark that grows to about two metres tall. The leaves or phyllodes are narrow wedge-shaped like an inverted triangle to about 5 mm long.
